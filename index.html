Meet the alien
Elliot is a set of powerful extensions to Sylius - the open-source e-commerce framework widely appreciated for its flexibility. This means you can easily build a unique ticketing experience for your fans and guests. 
Benefit from all the power of standardized e-commerce technology - manage products, process orders, set promotions, and configure all your online store details. Mix selling tickets with other items like your conference’s merch, and more.
Exactly like Sylius, Elliot is 100% based on Symfony - a free, open-source, high-performance PHP framework to build web applications, and APIs (with API Platform!). It’s free, robust, fast, secure, and loved by developers.
Coming from space, E.T.(E.) is familiar with clouds. So is his friend Elliot, ready to fly with you both as PaaS based on Platform.sh, and simply as a package of cosmic code shipped with Private Packagist.

3 modules to rule it all!

Call for Papers

form and review UI, including: speaker’s data, abstract, suggested talk duration, type, and other preferences

Page Builder

based on UI elements with rich content, enhanced with agenda edition (hours, rooms, topics, tracks, speakers, abstracts, etc.)

Ticket Sales & Management

consisting of the following extensions:
cart - ability to provide ticket owners (yes, multiple) data like name, company, and diet preference
tickets are products in Sylius but also have a separate back office where you can browse, and edit them, incl. changing the owner
sale of merchandise together with different, date and type-related tiers of tickets
checkout - buy as a company to change the VAT calculation
integrated TPay payments (Mollie or Stripe are also planned)
integrated mailing app Email Labs to keep attendees notified
possible bulk send and export
QR code scanner - yes, you can log in via the mobile browser and scan your attendees’ tickets!
